Alyson and Mena | July 28, 2020

Alyson and Mena both graduated from the University of Waterloo (yay Engineering!) this year and faced an economic downturn and pandemic. 2020 has been a year full of surprises and 2020 graduates did not get to experience many different senior year events such as gradball, IRS and a convocation. I loved photographing these two beautiful people around University of Toronto's downtown campus!
