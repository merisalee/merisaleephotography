Sheryl and Matt | July 21, 2020

I have a TON of photos of these two but I just can't help it. They're so much fun to photograph! Sheryl has been one of my best friends since we were 10 years old and she is such a pleasure to take photos for. She is a natural in front of a camera and is absolutely gorgeous. Sheryl and Matt joined me while I took photos for Betty and Maanoj and we had so much fun together. While I was taking photos for Betty and Maanoj, we would notice Sheryl and Matt goofing around the beach and dancing/singing to one another. They truly only have eyes for each other. The post wedding bliss is so real with these two and I think it really shows in these photos!
